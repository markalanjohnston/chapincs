Self-paced classroom material from Chapin High School Computer Science.

About

Each track is a sequence of self-paced lessons designed to be used in any introductory CS class — Fundamentals of CS, AP CSP, AP CSA, coding clubs, or a one-off Hour of Code. Lessons are written for students to work through on their own, with predict-then-check prompts, copy-able code, and a small deliverable at the end of each.
